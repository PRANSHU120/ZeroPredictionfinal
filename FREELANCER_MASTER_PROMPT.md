# MASTER PROMPT – Zeropredection Trading SaaS Website

Act as a Senior Software Engineer, Senior UI/UX Designer, and Frontend Engineer. Modify and improve the existing trading website into a premium, modern, fully responsive SaaS website for the trading service brand called Zeropredection.

## Project Context
This is a fintech/trading SaaS platform, not a gambling or flashy trading site. The core goal is to help users explore an additional income source without spending daily trading time, using a disciplined, system-driven strategy.

## Brand Details
- Domain: zeropredection.com
- Strategy Name: SwingNifty
- Platform: Tradetron
- Brand tagline: Data-Driven Trading. Disciplined Growth. Zero Prediction.

## Meaningful WHY / HOW / WHAT Structure

### WHY — Purpose of Zeropredection
Meaning: Explain the reason this product exists.
Copy direction: We are building this to help busy Indian investors explore an additional income source without active daily screen time, while keeping the messaging transparent, disciplined, and risk-aware.

### HOW — System & Execution Method
Meaning: Explain the method and operating process.
Copy direction: We use a disciplined trading system with rule-based entries, planned exits, risk-first execution, and continuous adaptation to changing market conditions.

### WHAT — SwingNifty Offering
Meaning: Explain the exact product/service being offered.
Copy direction: SwingNifty is a Nifty Futures swing trading strategy available through Tradetron subscription using the strategy link.

## Target Audience
Design for Indian retail investors, working professionals, beginners in trading, and mobile-first users who need trust, clarity, and simplicity.

## Design Direction
Create a premium fintech SaaS experience with a clean white background, deep green accents, soft mint gradients, dark charcoal text, subtle trading chart patterns, glassmorphism cards, rounded corners, soft shadows, and professional animations.

Avoid flashy crypto UI, gambling-like visuals, neon colors, and heavy animations.
